We are in the final stages of the CeylonEats Admin Panel development. The core infrastructure, vendor management, and financial moderation systems are fully operational. Our current focus is on final compliance checks and automated payout workflows.

1

Security & Access Infrastructure

Completed

Multi-Role Access

Created specialized dashboards for Owners, System Admins, and Vendor Managers, ensuring everyone sees only what they need.

Audit Logging

Every administrative action is tracked with a permanent record (who, what, when) for maximum accountability.

Spam Protection

Integrated Google security systems to prevent automated bot attacks and unauthorized login attempts.

Data Safety

Established a secure database foundation with industry-standard encryption and protection protocols.

2

Partner & User Management

Completed

Partner Onboarding

A streamlined workflow for restaurants and vendors to join the platform with manual approval stages.

Verification System

Internal tools to verify business details and documents before a vendor goes live on the platform.

User Control Center

Comprehensive tools to manage staff accounts, handle password resets, and suspend accounts if needed.

Status Monitoring

Real-time visibility into vendor health, allowing admins to pause or activate partners instantly.

3

Content Moderation & Sales

Completed

Listing Approval

A "Review Queue" for new menu items and restaurant listings to ensure they meet CeylonEats quality standards.

Category Management

Flexible tools to organize dining categories and tags, making it easy for customers to find food.

Financial Visibility

Detailed dashboards showing gross sales, platform fees, and net vendor earnings for every booking.

Advanced Filters

Search through thousands of transactions by date, partner name, or customer details effortlessly.

Completed

System-wide ability to set platform commissions that apply automatically to every transaction.
